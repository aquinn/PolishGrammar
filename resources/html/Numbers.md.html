Numbers

  • Numbers behave like adjectives depending on the case. See the Adjectives section for the endings.

  • The number 1 takes the same endings as ten ta to (this/that), hence jeden koń (one horse), jedna krowa (one cow), jedno drzewo (one tree).

  • Numbers 2, 3, 4. In Nominative-case uses the numbers 2 (masc/neuter. dwa, f. dwie, masc.pers. dwaj), 3 (trzy, masc.pers. trzej), 4 (cztery, masc.pers. czterej) take the plural of the noun: dwa konie (two horses), dwie krowy (two cows), dwaj chłopcy (two boys).

  • Numbers five and above are followed by the Genitive plural of the counted noun, as though one were saying ‘five of horses’ and so on. Pięć koni (five horses), sześć krów (six cows), siedem zeszytów (seven notebooks) and dziesięciu chłopców (10 boys)
